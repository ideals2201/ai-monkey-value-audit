How to choose the three journeys

Choose end-to-end business outcomes, not individual apps. Useful boundaries look like:

  • Inbound inquiry → qualification → human handoff
  • Qualified lead → CRM record → appointment
  • Form submission → validation → destination system
  • Order or payment event → fulfillment → customer notification
  • Document intake → extraction → human approval

Examples define the shape of an acceptable journey; they do not add platform-specific implementation to the audit.

Safe intake

Start with public, synthetic, or fully sanitized material: the business outcome, trigger, expected output, current tools, approximate monthly volume, and known failure points.

Do not send passwords, OTPs, API keys, cookies, production credentials, payment-card data, wallet secrets, KYC or tax documents, unredacted customer records, private screenshots, or raw production exports.
